PERFORMANCE

Here you can find some of the competences of our team in the area of Performance

ANALYSIS AND SIMULATION

With the growing need for safer and more sustainable buildings, it has become crucial for architects to take building performance into account during the development of their projects, especially during the initial design stages. Nowadays, analysis tools can help architects make design decisions informed by building performance, having become important tools for the design process. Using the tools developed by our team, we seek to make analysis tools easier, faster and more convenient to use.

LIGHTING ANALYSIS
Lighting conditions can have a significant impact on visual comfort and work productivity.
ENERGY ANALYSIS
Energy analysis should be a crucial concern to ensure both thermal comfort and energy efficiency of a building.
STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS
Structure plays an important role in a building's physical integrity. Ensuring structural requirements leads to more robust and safe constructions.
EVACUATION SIMULATION
Evacuation simulations can help architects understand and improve the safety measures of a building and avoid crowd disasters in case of an emergency.

OPTIMIZATION

Optimization, in the context of architectural design, can be defined as the search for the best performing design from a set of different solutions, according to certain criteria. Our approach to optimization relies on algorithms to automatically search for the optimal version of a design amongst many. By letting performance guide the design process of a project to achieve better performing solutions, we can reach Performance-Based Design.
